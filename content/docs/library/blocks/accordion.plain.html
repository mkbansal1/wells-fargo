How much home can I afford?

Many people start by determining what they can afford as a monthly payment. A common starting point is to calculate 25% of your gross monthly income.

What parts of my finances does a lender review?

A lender will check your credit score and history, your debt-to-income ratio, and take a general look at how much money you have in checking and savings accounts.

What is the minimum down payment?

Several low down payment options are available, including conventional loans with as low as 3% down.
