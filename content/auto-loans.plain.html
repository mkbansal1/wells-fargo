Answers to auto loan questions

How do I apply for a Wells Fargo auto loan?
Wells Fargo auto loans are only available through our nationwide network of nearly 11,000 dealerships. Be sure to ask your dealership if Wells Fargo auto financing is available. If your loan is funded by Wells Fargo Auto, we'll service your account.
How can I start managing my auto loan account online?
Start by setting up your account. You'll need your Social Security number and date of birth to get started. Once you have a username and password, you can use the Wells Fargo Mobile app or Wells Fargo Online® to make one-time payments, set up Autopay, access online statements, or manage other Wells Fargo accounts.Opens a modal dialog for footnote 1
How can I stop receiving paper statements for my auto loan?
Sign on to your account and select Manage delivery preferences. You can access up to 12 months of statements online in Statements and Documents.
How can I change my auto loan payment due date?

Before you apply for a due date change, you'll need to meet the following criteria:

  • You've made the first payment on your auto loan.
  • Your requested payment due date shouldn't be more than 15 days from your existing payment due date. For example, if your existing due date is the 5 th of the month, you can change it to the 20 th of the month, or any day in between.
  • This is your first payment due date change. (You're allowed one payment due date change for the entire term of the loan.)
  • Your account is current or no more than 10 days past due.

If you change your due date, your loan maturity date will be adjusted. Your monthly payment amount will remain the same.

To request a due date change, please call us at 1-800-289-8004.
